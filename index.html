EXPLORE THE BIOMES

The Forest Biome

Forest biome is characterized by a high density of trees and diverse flora and fauna, covering approximately 30% of the Earth's land surface.

Click to learn more

The Aquatic Biome

Aquatic biome is a diverse ecosystem that covers most of the Earth's surface, consisting of freshwater and saltwater environments and supporting a wide range of plant and animal life.

Click to learn more

The Desert Biome

The desert biome is an arid region with minimal rainfall, characterized by high temperatures during the day and cold temperatures at night.

Click to learn more

The Tundra Biome

Tundra biome is a cold, treeless region with permafrost, characterized by a short growing season and a limited variety of plant and animal life.

Click to learn more

The Grassland Biome

Grassland biome is characterized by vast expanses of grasses and a few scattered trees, with a diverse array of herbivores and predators.
